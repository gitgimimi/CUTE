CHIIKAWA໒꒱

Chiikawa is a small and cute creature from a Japanese comic created by Nagano. The name comes from “chiisai” (small) and “kawaii” (cute).

Chiikawa lives in a mysterious world with other characters like Hachiware and Usagi. Even though the world seems soft and adorable, it often includes scary or sad moments. Chiikawa is sweet, kind, and always trying hard. That’s why so many people love and support this tiny character.

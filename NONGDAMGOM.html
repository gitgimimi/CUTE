NONGDAMGOM

Nongdamgom is a strange and funny character who appears in the Chiikawa universe. He often shows up suddenly and tells weird jokes or makes unexpected comments.

His jokes are usually random and hard to understand, but they somehow make people laugh. With his round eyes and simple face, he brings a unique kind of humor to the story. Joke Bear may seem silly, but that’s what makes him charming.
